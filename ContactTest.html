Leadership

President | Chinma Uche

Math and Computer Science Teacher at CREC’s Academy of Aerospace and Engineering and the Greater Hartford Academy of Math and Science. Chinma chairs membership meetings and communicates with the CSTA leadership. She is also a member of the CSTA Leadership Cohort.

Vice President | James Veseskis

Computer Science Teacher at Hartford Magnet Trinity College Academy. James is responsible for chairing membership meetings in the absence of the President and communicates with CSTA leadership.

 

Secretary | Pauline Lake

Teaching Consultant for the Mobile Computer Science Principles Project at Trinity College. Pauline is responsible for taking meeting minutes and maintaining the content of the web site.

 

Treasurer | Betsy Dillard

Computer Science Teacher at Tolland High School. Betsy is responsible for maintaining and informing CTCSTA on the financial status of the group.

 

Librarian | Bradley Kjell

Professor and Chair of Computer Science, CCSU. Brad supervises the organization and management of Chapter information services and materials.
