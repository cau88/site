ExerciseU
For the 2013 Technovation Challenge, the 5 member team that I led created, did market analysis, and pitched our app ExerciseU, which won Honorable Mention (top 20) status in the international competition. Our team was later invited to the 2013 AppInventor Summit at MIT to present our work. ExerciseU was created to combat a sedentary lifestyle for people with busy schedules (ex. college students, busy mothers, etc.) My role on the team consisted of completely programming the app in AppInventor and doing market research.
